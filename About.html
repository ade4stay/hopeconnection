About Us

Hope Connection is a website designed to help homeless people in the United States who are in need of clothing, food, or toiletries, but are unable to find out places that could provide them with these necessities.

How Many Americans are Affected by Homelessness

On a single night in January 2016, a total of 549,928 Americans were homeless. Out of those 549,928 homeless people:

- 194,716 were in families, including children.
- 358,422 were single adults.
- 77,486 were people with disabling conditions.
- 40,000 were veterans.

How Does It Help the Homeless?

On our website, you can use our Shelter Locator or our Supplies Locator. With the Shelter Locator, you simply place in your address to find shelters nearby. If you are unable to reach them for some reason, then there will be a list of nearby safe places you can stay as well. With our Supplies Locator, you can place in your address and a choice between clothing, food, and toiletries. Afterwards, it will show you a list of places and what category of supplies they provide.
